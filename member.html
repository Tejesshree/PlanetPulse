    Q2. What are the types of Flasks available online?

    When shopping online for a flask water bottle, you’ll usually find two main types: insulated and non-insulated. Insulated flasks are ideal if you want your beverage to stay at the same temperature throughout the day.


    Non-insulated flasks are designed to carry your light weight flask anywhere.

    Q3. How does Milton flask work?

    A Milton flask is a water bottle that keeps the temperature constant for long hours. It is the double walled Vacuum Insulated that prevents heat transfer from outside, preserving the temperature inside and keeping the beverage hot and cold for 24 hours.     Milton flask's vacuum insulation ensures you can enjoy your favorite drinks at their preferred temperatures for extended periods. Each Milton flask has an ergonomic design, making it easy to carry around.

    Q4. Which metal is used in Milton's bottle?

It is designed to serve as an eco-friendly, reusable solution for people who want to carry water with them on the go.
